PhD Research

Current Focus

Studying How Learners Perceive Support in Distance and Online Education

My doctoral research at Geeta University investigates the critical factors that shape learner perception and effectiveness of support systems in Open and Distance Learning (ODL) environments.

Research Objectives

  • Analyzing learner perception of academic support services in ODL contexts
  • Evaluating effectiveness of online counseling and learner support mechanisms
  • Investigating post-pandemic shifts in distance education support requirements
  • Developing frameworks for ICT-based learner support systems
  • Understanding barriers and enablers in distance learning environments

Methodology

This research employs a mixed-methods approach combining quantitative surveys of ODL learners with qualitative interviews and case studies. The study draws on educational psychology, instructional design theory, and information systems research to provide comprehensive insights into learner support effectiveness.
